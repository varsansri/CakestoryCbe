About Us

Where Passion Meets Profession

Founded in 2018, CakestoryCbe Studio has trained over 1,200 students in the fine art of cake decoration. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned baker looking to refine your skills, our hands-on curriculum meets you where you are.

We believe cake decorating is equal parts science and creativity. Our instructors bring decades of combined industry experience — from Michelin-starred pastry kitchens to winning international competitions. Every class is limited to 8 students so you get the attention you deserve.
